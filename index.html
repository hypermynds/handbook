The Hypermynds Employee Handbook

Welcome to Hypermynds.

This handbook is the primary reference for anyone working at Hypermynds.
Its goal is to make every team member able to work effectively, safely and independently in a company that operates complex technology in regulated energy markets.

Here you will find:

  • practical information on how to set up and use your work tools
  • rules and processes that govern daily work
  • guidelines on how we build, deploy and operate our software
  • references on how we interact with colleagues, customers and partners

The handbook is a living document: it evolves together with the company.
